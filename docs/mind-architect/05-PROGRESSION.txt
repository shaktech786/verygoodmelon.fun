================================================================================
                          MAP SYSTEM
================================================================================

Each floor has a procedurally generated map with branching paths.
Players choose their route, creating different experiences each run.

MAP LAYOUT:
```
                    [BOSS]
                    /    \
                 [?]      [E]
                /   \    /   \
             [B]   [R]  [B]   [?]
              |     |    |     |
           [B]─[S]─[B]─[R]─[B]─[B]
              |     |    |     |
             [?]   [B]  [S]   [B]
                \   |    |   /
                 [START]
```

NODE TYPES:

[B] BATTLE NODE
───────────────
Standard bias encounter.
Rewards: Card choice (pick 1 of 3), gold.
Frequency: ~50% of nodes.

[E] ELITE NODE
──────────────
Harder enemy, better rewards.
Rewards: Rare card choice, relic, more gold.
Frequency: ~10% of nodes.
Marked with skull icon.

[R] REST NODE
─────────────
Heal or upgrade.
Options:
- MEDITATE: Restore 30% Coherence
- REFINE: Upgrade a card
- REFLECT: Remove a Flaw
Frequency: ~15% of nodes.

[S] SHOP NODE
─────────────
Spend gold on cards, relics, services.
Stock: 5 cards, 2 relics, 3 services.
Frequency: ~10% of nodes.

[?] MYSTERY NODE
────────────────
Random event (see Events section).
Could be good, bad, or choice-based.
Frequency: ~15% of nodes.

[T] TREASURE NODE
─────────────────
Free relic, no combat.
Rare, only 1-2 per floor.
Often requires difficult path.

================================================================================
                         RELIC SYSTEM
================================================================================

Relics are permanent passive bonuses for the run.

RELIC SLOTS: Unlimited (but harder to get later)

RELIC SOURCES:
- Elite battles: Choose 1 of 3
- Boss battles: Choose 1 of 3 (rare)
- Treasure nodes: 1 random relic
- Shop: Purchase for gold
- Events: Chance to find

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STARTER RELICS (One per school)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RATIONALIST: "Descartes' Meditation"
- Start each battle with +1 TP
- "I think, therefore I am prepared."

EMPIRICIST: "Hume's Impression"
- Evidence cards deal +1 damage
- "All knowledge begins with experience."

PRAGMATIST: "James's Cash Value"
- Cards that "work" in battle gain +0.5x next time
- "Truth is what works."

SKEPTIC: "Pyrrhonian Suspension"
- Start with "Doubt" card in hand (free negation)
- "I withhold judgment."

ABSURDIST: "Sisyphus' Boulder"
- When at <10 Coherence, deal +50% damage
- "One must imagine Sisyphus happy."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
COMMON RELICS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

"The Philosopher's Stone"

"Wittgenstein's Ladder"

"Aristotle's Golden Mean"

"Socrates' Gadfly"

================================================================================
                          EVENT SYSTEM
================================================================================

Events occur at Mystery nodes. They present choices with trade-offs.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SAMPLE EVENTS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

EVENT: "THE ALLEGORY OF THE CAVE"
─────────────────────────────────
You find prisoners watching shadows on a wall. They believe the shadows
are reality. Do you:

[A] Free them (Lose 10 Coherence, gain rare card "Enlightenment")
[B] Join them (Gain 5 Coherence, add "Illusion" flaw)
[C] Leave silently (No effect)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

EVENT: "THE SHIP OF THESEUS"
────────────────────────────
A ship has had every plank replaced. Is it the same ship?

[A] "Yes, identity persists" (Transform your oldest card into newest)
[B] "No, it's a new ship" (Remove 2 cards, add 2 random cards)
[C] "The question is meaningless" (Remove this event from future runs)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

EVENT: "BURIDAN'S DONKEY"
─────────────────────────
A donkey stands between two identical bales of hay, unable to choose,
and starves. You face two identical treasure chests. You may only take one.

[A] Take the left chest (Random reward)
[B] Take the right chest (Same random reward)
[C] Take neither (Gain "Decisiveness" card - always choose freely)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

EVENT: "THE EXPERIENCE MACHINE"
───────────────────────────────
A machine offers perfect simulated happiness. All your desires fulfilled,
but none of it real. Do you plug in?

[A] Yes (Full Coherence heal, but -10 max Coherence permanently)
[B] No (Gain "Authentic Living" card, +5 max Coherence)
[C] Destroy it (Gain gold, some players might have wanted it)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

EVENT: "PASCAL'S WAGER"
───────────────────────
You meet a gambler who offers a bet: Believe in something unprovable
for potentially infinite reward, or don't and risk missing out.

[A] Take the wager (Add "Leap of Faith" card, risky but powerful)
[B] Refuse (Add "Calculated Risk" card, consistent but modest)
[C] Question the premise (Add "Question" card, challenge assumptions)


================================================================================
                      META-PROGRESSION
================================================================================

Progress persists between runs, creating long-term goals.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
UNLOCKABLE CONTENT
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SCHOOLS OF THOUGHT (Character Classes):
- RATIONALIST: Unlocked from start
- EMPIRICIST: Complete Floor 1 with any school
- PRAGMATIST: Complete Floor 2 with any school
- SKEPTIC: Complete the game once
- ABSURDIST: Die 10 times (embrace failure)

NEW CARDS (Added to card pool):
- Complete specific achievements
- Defeat specific bosses
- Discover through events
- Total: ~120 cards, ~30 at start, rest unlocked

NEW RELICS (Added to relic pool):
- Defeat elite enemies
- Complete challenge runs
- Find secrets

NEW EVENTS:
- Encounter philosophical figures
- Make certain choices
- Explore all paths

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
INSIGHTS (Meta Currency)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Earned from:
- Completing runs: 10-50 Insights
- Achievements: 5-20 Insights
- Daily challenges: 10 Insights
- New discoveries: 5 Insights

Spent on:
- Unlock cards early: 20-50 Insights
- Unlock schools: 100 Insights
- Cosmetics: Variable
- Hints/tips: 5 Insights

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ACHIEVEMENT SYSTEM
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

COMPLETION ACHIEVEMENTS:
- "First Thoughts" - Complete Floor 1
- "Deeper Questions" - Complete Floor 2
- "Enlightened" - Complete the game
- "Mastery" - Complete with all schools

CHALLENGE ACHIEVEMENTS:
- "Minimalist" - Win with ≤15 cards
- "Maximalist" - Win with ≥40 cards
- "Pacifist" - Win using only questions
- "Speedrunner" - Win in <20 minutes
- "Perfect Logic" - Win without Coherence loss
- "The Five Whys" - Chain 5 Why? cards
- "Synthesis Master" - Create 10 Syntheses in one run

DISCOVERY ACHIEVEMENTS:
- "Collector" - Own every card (across runs)
- "Philosopher" - Encounter all events
- "Scholar" - Read all lore entries

PHILOSOPHICAL ACHIEVEMENTS:
- "Steelman Champion" - Win 10 fights using enemy logic
- "Devil's Advocate" - Win arguing FOR the bias
- "Amor Fati" - Achieve transcendent victory


================================================================================
                     DAILY CHALLENGES
================================================================================

Each day offers a special challenge run with:
- Fixed seed (everyone gets same run)
- Modifiers (harder or weirder rules)
- Leaderboard (score comparison)

SAMPLE MODIFIERS:
- "Socratic Mode": Can only play Question cards
- "Tabula Rasa": Start with 0 cards, must build everything
- "Heraclitus": All cards change each turn
- "Parmenides": No cards can change
- "Nihilist": -1 Coherence per turn, can you find meaning?

SCORING:
- Floor reached × 100
- Cards played × 1
- Enemies defeated × 10
- Coherence remaining × 2
- Bosses defeated × 500
- Flaws in deck × -20


================================================================================
                      NEW GAME+ MODE
================================================================================

After completing the game, unlock harder modes:

PHILOSOPHER+ (First NG+):
- Enemies have +20% HP
- Flaw cards are more punishing
- New elite enemies
- Alternate boss dialogue

SAGE++ (Second NG+):
- Enemies have +40% HP
- Start with a Flaw
- Limited card rewards
- True final boss unlocked

ENLIGHTENED+++ (Third NG+):
- Maximum difficulty
- No relics allowed
- Must maintain 80%+ Coherence
- True ending unlocked


================================================================================
                         SAVE SYSTEM
================================================================================

RUNS:
- Auto-save after every node
- Can quit and resume anytime
- One active run per save slot
- Three save slots

META:
- Progress saved automatically
- Cloud sync across devices
- Cannot be reset (permanent progress)

SETTINGS:
- Separate from save data
- Accessibility options persist
