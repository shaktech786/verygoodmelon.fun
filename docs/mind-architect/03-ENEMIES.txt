                    ENEMY (BIAS) DESIGN
================================================================================

================================================================================
                       ENEMY PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

Every enemy is a real cognitive bias. Fighting them teaches players to
recognize and overcome these patterns in real life.

DESIGN PRINCIPLES:
1. Behavior reflects actual bias pattern
2. Weakness matches real-world counter
3. Flaw cards represent how bias corrupts thinking
4. Defeating bias = understanding it

ENEMY CATEGORIES:
- CONFIRMATION BIASES: Seek supporting evidence
- LOGICAL FALLACIES: Broken reasoning patterns
- EMOTIONAL BIASES: Feeling-based distortions
- SOCIAL BIASES: Other-people-based errors
- MEMORY BIASES: Past-based distortions


================================================================================
                    FLOOR 1: FOUNDATION BIASES
================================================================================
Simpler patterns, teach basic mechanics.
Entrenchment: 20-40 HP

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CONFIRMATION BIAS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"I only see what I already believe."

VISUAL: A figure with tunnel vision, surrounded by discarded contrary evidence.

ENTRENCHMENT: 35
RESISTANCES: Evidence Resistance +2 (for confirming evidence)

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Selective Attention  │   5    │ Your highest-weight Evidence is discarded. │
│ Echo Chamber         │   3    │ Gains +2 Resistance for 2 turns.           │
│ Belief Shield        │   0    │ Immune to damage unless you play diverse   │
│                      │        │ card types (Evidence + Logic + Framework). │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

PATTERN:
Turn 1: Belief Shield
Turn 2: Selective Attention
Turn 3: Echo Chamber
Turn 4: Attack (8 damage)
Repeat

WEAKNESS:
- Diverse evidence deck (different sources)
- Playing contradicting evidence first (shocks it)
- Steelmanning (using its own logic)

FLAW ADDED: "Tunnel Vision" - One card type deals -2 damage.

COUNTER-STRATEGY:
Don't rely on one evidence type. Mix Observation + Testimony + Statistics.
The bias can't dismiss all sources.

EDUCATIONAL NOTE:
"Confirmation bias makes us seek information that confirms what we already
believe, while ignoring contradicting evidence. Combat it by actively
seeking disconfirming information."

AVAILABILITY HEURISTIC
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"If I can think of an example, it must be common."

VISUAL: A brain overflowing with vivid images, pushing out dry statistics.

ENTRENCHMENT: 25
RESISTANCES: None (but special mechanic)

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Recently played cards deal less damage. It "adjusts" to what's available.
Card damage reduced by -1 for each time played this battle.

│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Vivid Memory         │   6    │ Your Anecdote cards get +3 weight (trap!). │
│ Recency Flood        │   4    │ Last 3 cards you played go to bottom of    │
│                      │        │ draw pile.                                 │
│ Frequency Illusion   │   3    │ One random card appears 3x in your hand.   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Statistics and base rates
- Playing varied cards (no repetition)
- Meta-Analysis (counters recency)

FLAW ADDED: "Recency Bias" - Newest card in hand costs +1 TP.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANCHORING BIAS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"First number I heard must be right."

VISUAL: A heavy anchor chained to a brain, pulling it down.

ENTRENCHMENT: 30
RESISTANCES: Logic Resistance +1

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Locks onto the first card you play each turn. That card type deals
-50% damage for the rest of the fight unless broken.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Set the Anchor       │   0    │ Your first card becomes "anchored."        │
│ Weight of First      │   5    │ Anchored card type deals -50% damage.      │
│ Stubborn Reference   │   7    │ If you play 3+ of anchored type, stun self.│
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Opening with "Not" card (negates anchor)
- Diverse first-card choices
- Reframe card (changes card type)

FLAW ADDED: "Fixed Reference" - First card each turn costs +1 TP.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DUNNING-KRUGER EFFECT
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"I know everything about this topic I just learned."

VISUAL: A small figure casting a massive, confident shadow.

ENTRENCHMENT: 15 (thinks it's 60)
RESISTANCES: Immune to Doubt effects

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
HP bar shows 60, but real HP is 15. Overconfident attacks leave openings.
When "defeated" at fake 0, reveals true HP (already at 15).

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Peak of Mt. Stupid   │  10    │ BUT you get +1 TP next turn (overconfident)│
│ Dismiss the Experts  │   6    │ Expert Opinion cards deal 0 damage.        │
│ I Knew That          │   4    │ Copies your last card (badly—half effect). │
│ Supreme Confidence   │   0    │ Gains 5 fake Resistance (doesn't work).    │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Just attack consistently (it's weak despite bravado)
- Wait for openings after big attacks
- Humility cards deal bonus damage

FLAW ADDED: "Overconfidence" - +1 to all card weights (seems good, but
activates enemy effects that punish high weight).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BANDWAGON EFFECT
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Everyone believes it, so it must be true."

VISUAL: A crowd all pointing the same direction, trampling evidence.

ENTRENCHMENT: 30
RESISTANCES: Testimony Resistance +3

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Gets stronger for each Testimony/Expert card you play.
+2 damage per social proof card used.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Popular Opinion      │  3+X   │ X = number of Testimony cards you've played│
│ Mob Mentality        │   8    │ Adds "Conformity" flaw to your deck.       │
│ Appeal to Masses     │   5    │ Your next solo card deals -3 damage.       │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Direct Evidence (Observation, Experiment)
- Independent thinking cards
- Avoid Testimony-heavy decks

FLAW ADDED: "Conformity" - Playing unique cards costs +1 TP.


================================================================================
                    FLOOR 2: CHALLENGE BIASES
================================================================================
More complex patterns, require synergy play.
Entrenchment: 40-70 HP

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SUNK COST FALLACY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"I've invested too much to quit now."

VISUAL: A figure buried under accumulated baggage, still adding more.

ENTRENCHMENT: 55
RESISTANCES: None initially (grows over time)

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Gets +1 Resistance every turn. Gains +2 damage every turn.
The longer the fight, the harder it becomes. End it fast.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Too Far In           │  4+T   │ T = turn number                            │
│ Accumulated Weight   │   0    │ Locks one of your cards (unusable).        │
│ Doubling Down        │  10    │ Self-damages for 5 (desperate).            │
│ Wasted Investment    │   6    │ Remove your most-played card from deck.    │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

PATTERN:
Escalating. Each turn is harder than the last.
Must be defeated in 4-5 turns or becomes nearly impossible.

WEAKNESS:
- Decisive, high-damage openers
- First Principles (ignores past)
- Fresh Start cards (reset mechanics)

FLAW ADDED: "Past Investment" - Cards you've played 3+ times cost +2 TP.

EDUCATIONAL NOTE:
"Sunk costs are gone—they shouldn't influence future decisions. The only
relevant question is: what's the best choice going forward?"

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SURVIVORSHIP BIAS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Look at all the successful cases! (Ignore the failures.)"

VISUAL: A spotlight on winners, shadows hiding a graveyard of attempts.

ENTRENCHMENT: ??? (Hidden)
RESISTANCES: Evidence Resistance +2

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
HP is hidden. Cannot see true remaining Entrenchment.
Shows only successful "blocks" (when damage is negated).

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Success Stories      │   6    │ Hides your lowest-weight cards.            │
│ Visible Victories    │   4    │ Only your top 2 cards are visible.         │
│ Hidden Failures      │   0    │ Heals 10 HP (you can't see it happen).     │
│ Selection Effect     │   8    │ Strongest card deals 0 damage this turn.   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Statistics cards (reveal truth)
- Meta-Analysis (sees full picture)
- Base Rate cards (counter selection)

FLAW ADDED: "Success Blindness" - Can't see card weights until played.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FALSE DICHOTOMY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"It's either this or that. No other options exist."

VISUAL: A figure pointing at two options while a third door glows unnoticed.

ENTRENCHMENT: 45
RESISTANCES: Framework Resistance +2

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Each turn, forces you to choose between two options.
Often both options are bad. Can be escaped with certain cards.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Binary Choice        │   0    │ Choose: Discard hand OR lose 5 Coherence.  │
│ Excluded Middle      │   7    │ "Or" and "And" cards are disabled.         │
│ Forced Frame         │   5    │ Can only play 2 card types next turn.      │
│ Simplification       │   4    │ Removes all Framework cards from hand.     │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- "Or" card escapes false choices
- Lateral Thinking (finds third option)
- Reframe card (changes the frame)

FLAW ADDED: "False Dilemma" - Can only play 2 cards per turn.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STRAWMAN
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"That's not what they said, but this version is easier to attack."

VISUAL: A scarecrow version of an argument, being triumphantly destroyed.

ENTRENCHMENT: 25 (regenerates)
RESISTANCES: None

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
When "defeated," regenerates with 15 HP and distorts your deck.
Must be defeated 3 times OR killed with precise argument.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Misrepresent         │   4    │ Changes one card's text (worse effect).    │
│ Attack the Dummy     │   6    │ Deals damage based on YOUR weakest card.   │
│ Exaggerate           │   0    │ Doubles a card's weight (but makes fragile)│
│ Twist Words          │   5    │ Next card does opposite of intended.       │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

TRUE DEFEAT:
- Kill with Steelmanned argument (using its logic properly)
- Use "Clarification" card (prevents distortion)
- Hit with Photograph (cannot be distorted)

FLAW ADDED: "Misrepresentation" - One card's effect is permanently twisted.

================================================================================
                     FLOOR 3: MASTERY BIASES
================================================================================
Complex multi-phase patterns, test full understanding.
Entrenchment: 70-120 HP

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
COGNITIVE DISSONANCE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"These conflicting beliefs can both be true. Right? RIGHT?"

VISUAL: A brain split in two, each half arguing with the other.

ENTRENCHMENT: 100
RESISTANCES: Immune to single-type damage

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Has TWO health bars (50 each) that must be damaged simultaneously.
If you only damage one, it heals the other for 5 HP.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Split Mind           │  5+5   │ Two separate attacks, must block both.     │
│ Rationalization      │   0    │ Heals 15 HP on whichever bar is lower.     │
│ Uncomfortable Truth  │   8    │ Coherence damage equal to your deck size.  │
│ Denial Wave          │   6    │ All Evidence deals 0 damage next turn.     │
│ Belief Protection    │  10    │ Targets the card type you've used most.    │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Balanced decks that hit both bars
- Synthesis cards (resolve contradiction for massive damage)
- Dialectic (turns contradiction into weapon)

FLAW ADDED: "Inner Conflict" - Playing 2 of same type costs 3 Coherence.

ENTRENCHMENT: 80
RESISTANCES: Variable (increases with damage)

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Every time you deal damage, it gains Resistance to that damage type.
Strong Evidence makes it HARDER to defeat.

- Starting small (low-damage chip away)

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HINDSIGHT BIAS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"I knew it all along. (I didn't.)"

VISUAL: A figure looking at past through rose-tinted glasses, history rewriting itself.

ENTRENCHMENT: 75
RESISTANCES: Memory-type cards Resistance +5

SPECIAL MECHANIC:
Rewrites history of the battle. Can "undo" your past turns.
Discard pile becomes unreliable.

ATTACKS:
┌──────────────────────┬────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ NAME                 │ DAMAGE │ EFFECT                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rewrite History      │   0    │ Last turn's damage is undone.              │
│ I Knew It            │   7    │ Predicts your next card, +5 damage if right│
│ Obvious In Hindsight │   5    │ Removes "discovered" cards from deck.      │
│ False Memory         │   6    │ Shuffles cards from discard into deck.     │
│ Selective Recall     │   4    │ Your Memory cards add Flaws.               │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEAKNESS:
- Immediate-effect cards (can't undo)
- Recording cards (prevent rewrite)
- Present-focused reasoning

FLAW ADDED: "Creeping Determinism" - Past cards seem inevitable, lose flexibility.


================================================================================
                       ELITE ENEMIES
================================================================================
Mini-bosses that appear on hard paths. More complex, higher rewards.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE FUNDAMENTAL ATTRIBUTION ERROR
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Their failure is their fault. My failure is circumstance."

ENTRENCHMENT: 90
Has two modes: judging you (harsh) and defending self (gentle).

ATTACKS:
- When attacking: Hits hard, blames your "weakness"
- When defending: Excuses everything with situational factors

WEAKNESS: Consistency—treating self and other the same.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE JUST-WORLD FALLACY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Bad things happen to bad people. Good things happen to good people."

ENTRENCHMENT: 85
Gains massive damage when you have Flaw cards.

ATTACKS:
- "You Deserved It": Damage scales with your Flaws
- "Karma Accounting": Heals when dealing damage
- "Victim Blaming": Adds Flaw when you lose Coherence

WEAKNESS: Random-effect cards (chaos breaks just-world).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE HALO EFFECT
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"They're attractive, so they must be smart/kind/competent."

ENTRENCHMENT: 70
Buffs itself with every positive trait it "reveals."

ATTACKS:
- Stacks buffs that must be individually removed
- Your Evidence about it gets distorted by "aura"

WEAKNESS: Analysis cards that see through the halo.


================================================================================
                  DESIGN NOTES FOR ENEMIES
================================================================================

VISUAL DESIGN:
- Each bias should be immediately recognizable
- Color coding matches bias family
- Attack intents are always visible
- Damage numbers are clear

AUDIO:
- Each bias has signature sound
- Attack sounds reflect personality
- Defeat sounds are satisfying but not mean

DIALOGUE:
- Biases "speak" in character
- Dialogue reflects the bias pattern
- Defeat dialogue shows understanding
- Educational without being preachy
