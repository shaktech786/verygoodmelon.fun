                        BATTLE OVERVIEW

Combat in Mind Architect represents a philosophical debate or internal
struggle against cognitive bias. You're not fighting with swordsâ€”you're

================================================================================
                     ARGUMENT CHAIN MECHANICS
================================================================================

Cards played in sequence form an "Argument Chain."
Chains provide bonus damage based on length and coherence.

CHAIN LENGTH BONUS:
- 1-2 cards: No bonus
- 3 cards: +10% damage
- 4 cards: +25% damage
- 5 cards: +50% damage
- 6+ cards: +75% damage (maximum)

CHAIN COHERENCE:
Your chain must not contain contradictions. Playing cards that
logically oppose each other breaks coherence.

OPPOSING PAIRS (Cannot coexist without penalty):
- Absolute Truth + Relativism
- Determinism + Free Will (unless using Synthesis)
- Rationalism + Pure Empiricism
- Certainty + Radical Skepticism

COHERENCE BREAK PENALTY:
- Playing contradicting cards: -5 Coherence
- Coherence break interrupts chain: No chain bonus
- Some cards (Dialectic, Aufhebung) prevent penalty


================================================================================
                       COHERENCE SYSTEM
================================================================================

COHERENCE is your philosophical health.

STARTING COHERENCE: 50 (can be modified)
MAXIMUM COHERENCE: 75
LOSING CONDITION: Coherence reaches 0

GAINING COHERENCE:
- Rest sites: +10 Coherence
- Some relics: +1 per battle won
- Rare cards: Conditional coherence gain
- "Clarity" moments in narrative

LOSING COHERENCE:
- Enemy attacks: Variable damage
- Playing contradicting cards: -5 each
- Flaw card effects: Variable
- Some powerful cards cost Coherence

COHERENCE THRESHOLDS:
- 50+: Full effectiveness
- 30-49: Warning state (visual cue)
- 15-29: Critical (some cards disabled)
- 1-14: Desperate (simplified UI, tense music)
- 0: Defeat - "Lost in Confusion"

RESISTANCE TYPES:

- Your argument deals 20 Persuasion
- 15 of that was from Evidence weight
- Reduced: 20 - 3 = 17 actual damage

SYNTHESIS MECHANIC
â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€â”€
When you play Thesis on a card, then Antithesis on an opposing card,
they combine into a Synthesis.

Process:
1. Play "Thesis" on Card A (e.g., "Free Will")
2. Play Card A
3. Play "Antithesis"
4. Play Card B that opposes A (e.g., "Determinism")
5. SYNTHESIS triggers:
   - Damage: 3.0x multiplier applied
   - New Card: "Compatibilism" added to deck
   - No Coherence loss from contradiction

Synthesis cards are powerful and represent genuine philosophical progress.

================================================================================
                     VICTORY & DEFEAT CONDITIONS
================================================================================

VICTORY:
- Reduce enemy Entrenchment to 0
- Enemy admits defeat (dialogue)
- Rewards screen appears

DEFEAT:
- Coherence reduced to 0
- "Lost in Confusion" screen
- Run ends (unless revive relic)

SPECIAL VICTORIES:
- "Perfect Logic": Win without Coherence loss (+bonus)
- "Steelman Victory": Win using enemy's own logic (+2x rewards)
- "Socratic Victory": Win using only questions (+achievement)


================================================================================
                         DIFFICULTY SCALING
================================================================================

Battles scale based on floor and path choice:

FLOOR 1 (Foundation):
- Enemies have 20-40 Entrenchment
- Simple attack patterns
- Few Flaw additions
- Teaches basic mechanics

FLOOR 2 (Challenge):
- Enemies have 40-70 Entrenchment
- Complex patterns
- Multiple intents per turn
- Requires synergy play

FLOOR 3 (Mastery):
- Enemies have 70-120 Entrenchment
- Elite abilities
- Flaw combos
- Tests full understanding

BOSS SCALING:
- Floor 1 Boss: 100 Entrenchment
- Floor 2 Boss: 150 Entrenchment
- Floor 3 Boss: 200+ Entrenchment


================================================================================
                      STATUS EFFECTS
================================================================================

POSITIVE (On You):
- FOCUSED: +1 TP next turn
- INSPIRED: Draw +2 cards next turn
- GROUNDED: Immune to Coherence loss next turn
- INSIGHTFUL: See enemy deck for 1 turn
- ELOQUENT: All cards cost -1 TP

NEGATIVE (On You):
- CONFUSED: Random card costs +1 TP
- DOUBTFUL: -0.5x to all multipliers
- DISTRACTED: Draw -1 card next turn
- BIASED: Cannot play certain card types
- SILENCED: Cannot play Meta cards

ON ENEMY:
- EXPOSED: Takes +50% damage
- STUNNED: Skips next turn
- WEAKENED: Deals -3 damage
- VULNERABLE: No Resistance next turn
- ENLIGHTENED: Enemy joins you (special ending)
